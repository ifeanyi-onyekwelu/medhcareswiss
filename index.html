Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare solutions tailored to meet diverse needs with quality and precision.

Consultation and Assessment

MedHcareSwiss.SNC helps the patient understand their medical condition and treatment options. They may connect the patient with medical professionals or specialists to discuss their case and provide expert opinions.

Destination Selection

Based on the patient's medical needs and preferences, MedHcareSwiss SNC assists in selecting the most suitable destination and healthcare facility. They consider factors such as the facility's reputation, expertise, cost, and availability of specialized treatments.

Appointment Scheduling

MedHcareSwiss.SNC helps in arranging appointments with healthcare providers, including doctors, surgeons, and specialists. They coordinate schedules and ensure that the patient's medical needs are met efficiently.

Travel and Accommodation

MedHcareSwiss.SNC often handle the logistics of travel and accommodation for the patient and their accompanying companions. They may arrange flights, local transportation, and accommodation near the healthcare facility.

Medical Records and Reports

MedHcareSwiss.SNC facilitates the collection and transfer of medical records, test results, and other relevant documents between the patient's home country and the destination healthcare facility. This ensures that the treating physicians have a comprehensive understanding of the patient's medical history.

Language Interpretation

If there is a language barrier between the patient and the healthcare providers in the destination country, MedHcareSwiss SNC provide interpretation services to facilitate effective communication throughout the medical journey.

Concierge Services

MedHcareSwiss.SNC often offer additional support services such as local sightseeing, arranging leisure activities, and providing assistance with visa applications, if required.

Treatment Cost Estimates and Financing

MedHcareSwiss.SNC provides the patient with a detailed breakdown of the treatment costs, including medical procedures, consultations, hospital stays, and any additional services required. They may also assist in exploring financing options or insurance coverage, if applicable.

Post-Treatment Care

After the medical procedure or treatment, MedHcareSwiss SNC helps coordinate follow-up appointments and ensures that the patient receives appropriate post-treatment care. This may involve arranging rehabilitation or physical therapy sessions, if necessary
